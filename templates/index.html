To get insights we need data, so:

  1. 1 Open a WhatsApp chat.

    Group chats provide more insights

  2. 2 Open the Options Menu

    Looks like a 3 dotted button at the top right corner.

  3. 3 Click on More, then Export Chat

    You should get a pop up at this moment. Ensure to pick "without media"

  4. 4 Export your data.

    They're different ways to save to your device. Just get the file here, we'll do the rest
